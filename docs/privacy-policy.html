Tabuada do 10 Privacy Policy

Tabuada do 10 works locally on your Android device. It stores multiplication progress, answers, statistics, and notification settings on your device so it can schedule practice questions.

Data stored

The app stores practice progress such as shown counts, correct answers, wrong answers, recent attempts, learned status, and notification preferences.

Notifications

The app requests notification permission to show local multiplication questions. It requests boot permission to restore the local schedule after your device restarts or the app is updated.

Data sharing

The app does not send practice data or personal data to our servers. It does not include analytics, ads, third-party tracking, account creation, or a backend service.
